Care and cleaning

Self Clean

  • Turn Temperature Control Dial to OFF ( ) and set Adjustable Steam Switch to Dry Iron . Fill iron with water to 1/2 of its maximum capacity.
  • Turn Temperature Control Dial to Linen setting and let iron heat for 2 minutes. Keep Adjustable Steam Switch on Dry Iron .
  • Turn Temperature Control Dial to OFF ( ). Unplug and hold over a sink with soleplate facing down.
  • Turn Adjustable Steam Switch to MAX, while pressing Blast Button often. Boiling water and steam will flow out of steam vents. Allow all water to drain from iron.
  • Gently move iron front to back to allow water to clean entire soleplate area.
